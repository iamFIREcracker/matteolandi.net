Advent of Code solutions

My Advent of Code solutions, in Common Lisp.
  • All the code is availalbe on GitHub
  • 2018 was my first year of Advent of Code and I decided to use that as an excuse to learn Common Lisp; it was not easy (seriously, it was not); never got a single day solved in less than 24 hours (I joined the competition late and played catch-up instead of trying to solve the problem of the day); I got my 50th star somewhere in the middle of January 2019; the reason why the "2018" section down below does not have solution links for each day is that I originally had all of my solutions squeezed into a giant main.lispfile and I am still in the process of refactoring all this
  • I did 2017 after completing the 2018 edition
  • I was working from India during the 2019 edition, and also attended a wedding (300 Km far from where I was staying) during one of the weekends I was there; even though that made "complete all the problems by the 25th" resolution a tiny bit more difficult to fulfil, I was still able to get my 50th star before the end of that year (an improvement from the edition from 2018)
  • I did 2016 in preparation to the 2020 edition; this is when I got into literate programming and started to document my solutions
  • 2020 was my first year in which I managed not only to earn my 50th star on Dec 25, but also to solve each day's challenge in less than 24 hours
  • I completed 2015 right after the 2020 edition; this is the edition where I tried to be as descriptive as possible in my solutions write-up
