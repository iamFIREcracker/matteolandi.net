Resolutions

(Probably overcomplicated) New years resolutions tracker

Description

A web app that helps you keep track of your new year resolutions.

It reads data from a Google spreadsheet, and displays a calendar for each resolution you set for yourself, highlighting the days where you progressed (e.g. you read, you exercised), as well as the days where you reached your weekly goal (e.g. read more than 5 days a week, or exercised at least once every three days).

See it in action: 2018, 2017.
